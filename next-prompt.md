A premium pricing/subscription popup for a finance app with 3 distinct plans.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first
- Theme: Dark, premium, financial architect style
- Background: Deep Navy (#080B12 - --bg)
- Cards/Columns: Darker Navy (#141928 - --bg3) with subtle borders
- Primary Accent: AI Blue (#5B8DEF - --accent)
- Success Green: #2DD4A0 (--green)
- Text Primary: Off-white (#EEF0F8 - --text)
- Text Secondary: Gray-blue (#8892B0 - --text2)
- Borders: Low opacity white (rgba(255,255,255,0.12) - --border)

**Page Structure:**
1. **Header:** Centered title "Choose Your Plan" with a subtitle explaining why to upgrade.
2. **Pricing Grid:** 3 columns layout.
   - **Column 1 (Free):** 
     - Title: "Basic"
     - Price: "₹0"
     - Features: Max 2 accounts, 3 months history, 3 budgets, 10 categories.
     - Button: "Current Plan" (disabled/secondary).
   - **Column 2 (Pro Monthly):** 
     - Title: "Pro Monthly"
     - Price: "₹99/mo"
     - Badge: "Popular" (Glowing AI Blue).
     - Features: Unlimited accounts, Full history, Unlimited budgets, All premium features (Net Worth, Formal Loans, Reports, Exports).
     - Button: "Upgrade Now" (Primary accent).
   - **Column 3 (Pro Yearly):** 
     - Title: "Pro Yearly"
     - Price: "₹799/year"
     - Badge: "Best Value (33% Off)" (Green accent).
     - Features: Everything in Pro Monthly + Priority support.
     - Button: "Go Yearly" (Primary accent).
3. **Footer:** Secure payment icons and "Cancel anytime" notice.

**Specific Styles:**
- Use glassmorphism for badges.
- Use glowing effect for the "Popular" plan.
- Use checkmark icons for features.
- Ensure 3-column responsive grid.
